Case Study 1: Digital & Network Launchpad

Challenge: Decathlon Bangladesh lacked any unified digital platform for logistics data and had no scalable training system, causing inefficiencies and inconsistent practices across its growing network.

Approach: I designed and launched the first Decathlon BD logistics website, rolled out a Google-powered performance dashboard to 100+ teammates, and built the "Logistics Learning School" online academy with self-paced content.

Impact: Empowered over 100 team members with live data, established a certified training program that standardized best practices globally, and created the first-ever country-wide logistics website and learning platform.

Case Study 2: The Agile Supplier Transformation

Challenge: A $50M 3PL supplier network was driving up costs, onboarding new partners was slow, and performance tracking was manual—hindering accountability and savings.

Approach: I launched an Agile-based supplier onboarding program, developed a Tableau dashboard to centralize all supplier KPIs, and used dashboard insights to restructure logistics routes and renegotiate contracts.

Impact: Delivered a 15% reduction in annual logistics spend, cut supplier onboarding time by 30%, and unlocked $500K in annual savings through data-driven negotiations.

Assets & Related Links: The dashboard is proprietary, but you can learn more from the official Decathlon Sustainability Report.

Case Study 3: The Founder's Digital Blueprint

Challenge: Early-stage, mission-driven founders lacked a cohesive digital narrative, scalable service framework, and aligned go-to-market plan, resulting in low engagement and weak lead conversion.

Approach: I conducted SWOT and competitor research, crafted memorable service models (e.g., "The Rise Framework™"), and delivered end-to-end digital blueprints covering site flow, CTAs, and lead-magnet funnels.

Impact: Provided 3 comprehensive Digital Blueprints for e-commerce, coaching, and craft clients and equipped founders with a clear roadmap projected to increase average order value by 20%.

Assets & Related Links: PROJXON (Consulting Firm), Fly Fearless (Client), Celyfos (Client), Zephyr (Client).

Case Study 4: The Patient-Centric Scheduling System

Challenge: Therapprove's referral platform suffered from poor therapist discovery and a cumbersome booking process, leading to high no-show rates and low patient satisfaction.

Approach: I mapped user pain points, introduced location-based filtering with map views to improve search, and designed a streamlined calendar interface for easy availability setup and preview.

Impact: Increased patient-provider match accuracy by 25%, reduced scheduling time by 20%, and created a feedback loop that accelerated product-team decision-making.
